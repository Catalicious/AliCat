About

Alexandria Gowda is a registered nurse certified by the California Board of Registered Nursing. She did her Bachelor of Science in Nursing at the prestigious Southern Illinois University at Edwardsville.

Alex currently works alongside two of the top plastic surgeons in the bay area, where she has received extensive training in both surgical and non-surgical options. She has extensive knowledge in topics such as safety in dermal fillers, laser treatments, and complex injection techniques.

Alex is the Director of Nursing at Cheng Plastic Surgery. She has many years of experience in aesthetics. She is also certified in BOTOX® Cosmetic, the JUVÉDERM® family of fillers, the Restylane® family of fillers, Dysport®, Radiesse® and BELOTERO BALANCE®. In addition to injectables, she conducts SkinPen® microneedling and provides excellent skin care advice.
